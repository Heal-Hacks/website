Fly High

Parent Organization of Heal Hacks



Fly High has partnered with other non-profits across the nation, and has reached students from across the country through their discord platform. They aim to bridge the gap between under-served communities and access to healthcare and STEM learning worldwide through virtual workshops, speaker sessions, podcasts, and self-produced mobile applications.
