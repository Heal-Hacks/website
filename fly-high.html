Fly High

Parent Organization of Heal Hacks




Fly High has partnered with other non-profits across the nation, and has reached students from across the country through their discord platform. They aim to bridge the gap between under-served communities and access to healthcare and STEM learning worldwide through virtual hackathons like Heal Hacks, workshops, community-based literary publications, book donation drives, and more.
